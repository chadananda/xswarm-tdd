# Writing Voice
Applies to all human-facing text: docs, READMEs, blog posts, PR descriptions, commit messages, comments.

Concise, technical, slightly informal. Clarity and signal over politeness or padding.
Short sentences default; mix longer ones for rhythm — don't sound templated.
Direct, opinionated, slightly blunt = fine.

## Banned
Generic AI phrases: "great question", "let's dive in", "in conclusion", "overall", "on the other hand", "it's worth noting".
Corporate/marketing tone. Filler transitions. Restating what was just said.

## Word Choice
Plain > fancy: use not utilize, help not assist, start not embark, build not architect (as verb), show not demonstrate.
No hedging without reason — "might", "perhaps", "it could be argued" weaken signal.
